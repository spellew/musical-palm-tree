Rabbit

When they are super-happy, bunnies jump in the air and twist their bodies and heads in opposite directions. This sudden movement is called a binky.

  • Habitats: meadows, woods, forests, grasslands, deserts and wetlands
  • Diet: grass, forbs, and leafy weeds
  • Length: varies from eight to twenty inches
  • Weight: varies from less than a pound to more than ten

Rabbits are small, furry, mammals with long ears, short fluffy tails, and strong, large hind legs. They have 2 pairs of sharp front teeth, one pair on top and one pair on the bottom.
